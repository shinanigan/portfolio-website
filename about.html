About

My Story

I'm a UX UI designer with a background in psychology, international development studies and human rights. Helping people has always been my passion, but working with human rights and policy was often frustrating since I couldn't see the concrete results of my work. I soon realized that seeing the positive results and the actual product of my work in a more tangible way is an important element to me. Being able to solve problems with solid solutions derived from research,communication and iterations is one of the things that drew me to UX/UI design.

Just as I advocated for all people from various backgrounds in my human rights work, I aim to advocate for users in my UX work and to see the product of my work become a positive experience for them. I am a firm believer that my diverse educational and professional experience coupled with my international ventures strengthen my understanding of and interactions with users across the board and bring a fresh perspective to my work.
